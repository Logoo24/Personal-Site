About

A short biography of a curious student.

Hi, I'm Logan. I'm a college student, occasional writer, and full-time tinkerer. This site is where I keep the things I'd rather not lose to the bottom of my notes app.

What I'm doing right now

Studying, writing, and trying to figure out which of the many shiny things in front of me are worth chasing. I post about classes I'm taking, things I'm reading, and projects I can't stop thinking about.

What I care about

Clear writing. Honest argument. Ideas that survive contact with reality. I have a slight allergy to jargon and a soft spot for marginalia. I'd rather be wrong in interesting ways than right in boring ones.

What I'm working on

A handful of things, never all at once. Right now: this site, a long essay about something I shouldn't talk about until it's done, and the slow accumulation of class notes I plan to publish at the end of each semester.

"Writing is thinking. To write well is to think clearly. That's why it's so hard." - David McCullough

A few practical details

  • I read everything I'm sent at hello@loganbrandall.com.
  • I post when I have something worth posting - never on a schedule.
  • If you're a fellow student and want to swap notes on a class, please write.
